about=Q2G is a powerful QGIS plugin that streamlines the process of uploading layers and managing styles on GeoServer through a user-friendly interface. It allows users to upload QGIS layers to GeoServer in multiple formats, manage SLD (Styled Layer Descriptor) styles, and create and manage GeoServer workspaces with ease. The plugin also enables users to preview layers and styles before deployment, supports batch uploading of multiple layers at once, and works seamlessly with PostGIS, Shapefile, GeoPackage, and GeoTIFF formats.
